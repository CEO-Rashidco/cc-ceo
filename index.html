Project "Ahlni" (Empower Me)

I am proud to share one of the initiatives I managed and coordinated, "Ahlni". This initiative started as a creative idea of mine and transformed into a full-scale project at Blumont, responding to the urgent need for youth to enhance their skills for the labor market.

Through this project, I graduated three cohorts of talented youth. They received specialized training in negotiation, persuasion, and communication. I personally guided them on creating professional resumes and standout LinkedIn profiles, bridging the gap between education and employment.

The project yielded remarkable results, with participants achieving clear success in the job market. As the creator and director, I received substantial support from my managers to provide necessary resources. It wasn't just training; it was a career transformation journey.
